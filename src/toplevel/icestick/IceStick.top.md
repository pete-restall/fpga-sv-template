# The iCEstick Development Board

The top-level module for blinking an LED on the [iCEstick](https://www.latticesemi.com/en/Products/DevelopmentBoardsAndKits/iCEstick) Development Board.

This board has a 12MHz clock input and four red LEDs in a ring, along with a green LED in the centre.  Like the iCE Sugar board, we're breaking new ground by blinking the LEDs at different rates.

Half of the red LEDs don't even flash at the same time as the other half - it's like a disco on your bench.
